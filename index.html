About Me

Born a Hacker

Since I was little I've been playing with tech. In 7th grade I worked on Minecraft mod packs with other developers and made The Simple Pack which achieved 70k downloads last year. Recently I've gone to my first Hackathon where I learned to develop web apps.

User Experience

I enjoy making websites such as the one you're on right now. I love Google's material design and incorporate it into every interface I make. My main forcus at the moment is html, css, javascipt and python.
